I'm a first-year master student in School of Information, University of Michigan. My specialization is Human Computer Interaction (HCI) that we put users in the top priority when designing interaction experience. Products or services are designed for a reason. Therefore, existing problems have to be defined well first. This can be achieved by standing in users' shoes and looking from users' eyes instead of product perspectives. I'm aiming for becoming an User Experience (UX) designer who helps people use technology more comfortably and confidently.

I come from Taiwan and was graduated from National Taiwan University. My mother language is Chinese and I understand Taiwanese (dialect). I have also learned Japanese for 2 years, Spanish and Korean for a year. I really enjoy learning a new language which helps me understand a new culture more deeply and directly.
