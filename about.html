Food

Taiwan is famous for our delicious cuisine and night markets. Most of our food is salty and better tasted with rice or noodles. Some of my favorites include Braised Pork on Rice, Oyster Omelette, Milkfish Soup and Sesame Oil Chicken. Just to name a few. Since Taiwan is an island surrounded by ocean, seafood cuisine is very popular.

We have some sweet traditional snacks as well, such as Hakka Mochi and Wheel Pies.

Because we have subtropical climate, there are a wide variety of fruits. Lychee, sugar-apple and banana are my favorites.

Sports

I'm literally a sport fan! I love watch NBA and Tennis tournaments. Now as a student in University of Michigan, I'm proud to support the Wolverines, our Football team! GO BLUE!

I myself enjoy playing basketball, tennis and badminton a lot! I'm also a fast dash runner. No matter I am a watcher or player of a sport, it makes me feel alive. People are connected across the world by sports. Even we may not understand each other's language, we all go high and low with the games in the same way.

Music

Music is indispensable in my life, just like oxygen and water. Music adds so much color to the world. It leads me to new cultures and opens a door for me to learn new languages.

There are some artists always give me strength through their music when my brain is in a mess. Coldplay is such a good storyteller and a magician at the same time. I feel calm and touched whenever listening to their music.

Another is a Korean singer, Taeyang, from the group, Bigbang. His tender but soulful voice accompanied me through all struggles in my undergraduate. He may not be the most skilful singer, but definitely the most passionate one.
